Function gf2^7mult finds product of two elements of a field GF(27), a=a0+a1x+a2x2+a3x3+a4x4+a5x5+a6x6 and b=b0+b1x+b2x2+b3x3+b4x4+b5x5+b6x6 with the output, ab=c=c0+c1x+c2x2+c3x3+c4x4+c5x5+c6x6 written on the last 7 bits. Inputs a and b must remain unchanged.
