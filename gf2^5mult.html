Function gf2^5mult finds product of two elements of a field GF(25), a=a0+a1x+a2x2+a3x3+a4x4 and b=b0+b1x+b2x2+b3x3+b4x4 with the output, ab=c=c0+c1x+c2x2+c3x3+c4x4 written on the last 5 bits. Inputs a and b must remain unchanged.
