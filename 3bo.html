you are in... Main\3-bit optimal NCT circuits

This file contains NCT gate count optimal circuits for each of the 40,320 3-bit reversible functions. Such optimal implementations were first reported in quant-ph/0207001, however, our implementation may be different (there may be more than one optimal circuit for a given function, and depending on how the search is organized, it may find different optimal implementations).
